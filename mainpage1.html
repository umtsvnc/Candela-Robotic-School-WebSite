Who We Are

At Candela Robotic School, we are a community of educators, engineers, and dreamers who believe in the power of technology to shape a better future. Our mission is to provide engaging, hands-on learning experiences that empower students to become the next generation of tech innovators.

Through teamwork, creativity, and critical thinking, our students gain real-world skills in robotics, coding, and problem-solving. We’re not just teaching technology—we’re building the future.

Our Achievements

Caretta Beach Cleaning Robot

We have developed an eco-friendly robot powered by artificial intelligence that can detect and collect plastics, bottles, bags, and other waste on beaches. Inspired by Caretta, one of the most iconic sea creatures of the Mediterranean, this robot is designed to protect sea turtles’ nests and preserve their natural habitats.

EcoRobot Recycling Smart Bin Desing

EcoRobot provides innovative solutions using recycled materials. It teaches children coding and environmental awareness, while smart recycling bins offer a sustainable and cost-effective way to tackle pollution. We aim to expand this project on a larger scale in the future.
